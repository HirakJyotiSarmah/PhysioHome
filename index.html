TENS ( Transcutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulation )

Know Treatment

Treatment Details

Description

Transcutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulation (TENS) therapy in physiotherapy involves the use of a small, battery-operated device that delivers low-voltage electrical currents to targeted areas of the body. This treatment aims to alleviate pain by modulating nerve activity and influencing the perception of pain signals.

Treatment Overview

  • Utilizes a small, battery-operated TENS device, delivers low-voltage electrical currents to targeted areas of the body.
  • Alleviates pain by modulating nerve activity and influencing pain signal perception.

Ultrasound

Know Treatment

Treatment Details

Description

Ultrasound therapy in physiotherapy involves the application of high-frequency sound waves to targeted areas of the body. These sound waves generate mechanical vibrations that penetrate deep into the tissues, producing a warming effect. This process can stimulate blood flow, reduce inflammation, and promote tissue healing, making it a valuable tool in the treatment of various musculoskeletal conditions.

Traction

Know Treatment

Treatment Details

Description

Traction is a therapeutic technique employed in physiotherapy to address spinal and joint-related issues. It involves the application of a controlled pulling force to the spine or a specific joint, creating separation between vertebrae or joint surfaces. This gentle stretching helps alleviate pressure on nerves, reduce compression, and promote proper alignment.
